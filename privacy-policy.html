2.3 Remote Configuration Server

On each app launch, the App contacts our configuration server at 157.230.5.252 to fetch an updated config.json file. This request contains your IP address and basic device info. The config file controls ad unit IDs and UI parameters. No personal data is stored on this server beyond standard access logs.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • Reward Processing: Your email address is stored in Firebase Firestore and used solely to process reward redemption requests.
  • App Personalisation & Analytics: Firebase Analytics data helps us understand how users interact with the App so we can improve the experience.
  • Push Notifications: Your FCM token may be used to send re-engagement notifications. You can disable these in your device notification settings at any time.
  • Advertising: Your GAID may be shared with Google Ad Manager to serve personalised advertisements. See Section 5 for full details.
  • Remote Configuration: We use your IP address to serve the correct configuration file. This data is not combined with personal identifiers.
  • Legal Compliance: We may use or disclose information where required by applicable law.
We do not sell your personal data to any third party for their own marketing purposes.

5 Advertising & Ad Networks

The App displays advertisements served by Google Ad Manager. Ads may be personalised based on your interests using your Google Advertising ID (GAID).

5.1 Types of Ads Displayed

  • Banner Ads – displayed during quiz gameplay at the bottom of the screen.
  • Rewarded Video Ads – optional ads that users may watch to earn in-app Robux bonuses or to start a level.
  • Interstitial Ads – full-screen ads shown upon level completion.
  • App Open Ads – may be shown when the app is launched (configurable remotely).

5.2 Opting Out of Personalised Ads

You can limit ad personalisation by adjusting your device settings:

  • On Android 12 and below: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • On Android 13+: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID

5.3 Remote Ad Configuration

All ad unit IDs and placement rules are fetched from our server at launch. This allows us to update ad configuration without requiring an app update. No user data is transmitted in this configuration request beyond a standard HTTP GET.

6 Data Retention & Deletion

  • Email Address: Retained in Firebase Firestore for up to 90 days after your reward request has been processed, then deleted.
  • Firebase Analytics data: Retained per Google's standard retention policy (up to 14 months).
  • FCM Token: Retained until you uninstall the App or revoke notification permissions.
  • Local data (SharedPreferences): Stored on your device only. Cleared when you uninstall the App.

Data Deletion Request

To request deletion of your personal data (email address and Firebase records), contact us at kunal@thebeglobal.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request – Get Robux Reward". We will process your request within 30 days.

7 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Firebase Firestore security rules restricting unauthorised access.
  • HTTPS for all network communication with our config server and Firebase.
  • No storage of financial information or payment data.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8 Children's Privacy

Users between 13–17 should have parental consent before using the App.
