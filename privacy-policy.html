02 Information We Collect

To deliver its core functionality, the App collects the following information:

  • Email Address: Collected when you initiate a reward redemption request at Level 15. Stored in Firebase Firestore solely for the purpose of processing your reward.
  • Google Advertising ID (GAID): Accessed by Google Ad Manager for serving advertisements and by Firebase Analytics for analytics purposes.
  • Device & OS Information: Device model and Android version, used for crash reporting and compatibility checks.
  • App Session Data: Screen views and level completion events collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the App experience.
  • FCM Token: A push notification token issued by Firebase Cloud Messaging, used to send re-engagement notifications. You may disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • IP Address: Transmitted to our remote configuration server (157.230.5.252) on each app launch to fetch the latest ad and UI configuration. Not stored beyond standard server access logs.
  • In-App Progress: Level progress and virtual point balance stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences. Never transmitted to external servers.

We do not collect: passwords, payment information, contacts, precise location data, or any sensitive personal information.

03 How Your Data Is Used

  • Reward Processing: Your email address is used solely to process and fulfil reward redemption requests. It is stored securely in Firebase Firestore.
  • Push Notifications: Your FCM token is used to send re-engagement notifications. You can opt out via your device's notification settings at any time.
  • Advertising: Your GAID and device signals are used by Google Ad Manager to display advertisements within the App.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics uses session data and device info to help us understand how users interact with the App and improve its features.
  • Remote Configuration: Your IP address is used to serve the correct configuration file from our server. It is not combined with any personal identifiers.
  • Local Game State: In-app progress is cached locally on your device for a fast, offline-capable experience.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party for their own marketing purposes.

05 Advertising

This App displays advertisements served by Google Ad Manager. Ad types shown include Banner, Interstitial, Rewarded Video, and App Open ads. Google may use your device's Advertising ID and other non-personal signals to serve relevant advertisements.

All ad unit IDs and placement rules are fetched remotely from our configuration server at launch, allowing us to update ad settings without requiring an app update.

To opt out of personalised ads at any time, go to your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation (or on Android 13+, Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID).

06 Data Retention & Deletion

  • Email Address: Retained in Firebase Firestore for up to 90 days after your reward request is processed, then deleted.
  • Firebase Analytics Data: Retained per Google's standard policy (up to 14 months).
  • FCM Token: Retained until you uninstall the App or revoke notification permissions.
  • Local Progress Data: Stored on your device only. Cleared automatically when you uninstall the App.

To request deletion of your personal data, email kunal@thebeglobal.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request – RBX Rewards". We will process your request within 30 days.

07 Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including Firebase Firestore security rules restricting unauthorised access and HTTPS for all network communication with our configuration server and Firebase services. In-app progress data is stored locally on your device using standard Android SharedPreferences and is never transmitted externally.

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
